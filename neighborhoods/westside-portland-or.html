The Westside

Portland's leafy historic district at the foot of Forest Park

Forest, Bungalows, and Breweries

The Westside is Portland's quintessential established residential district — Craftsman and Foursquare bungalows lined up under Douglas fir canopies, with the eastern edge of Forest Park looking down on the neighborhood from above. Most blocks were laid out before World War I, and the architecture and tree canopy have been carefully preserved through historic-district zoning.

Daily life centers on the Northwest 23rd and Northwest 21st commercial strips, where independent coffee shops, bookstores, fromageries, and Michelin-recommended restaurants sit shoulder-to-shoulder in restored Victorians. Streetcar service connects to downtown, the Pearl District, and the South Waterfront in under twenty minutes.

Highlights
  • 5,000-acre Forest Park steps from your door
  • Historic-district zoning preserves architecture
  • Independent shopping & dining on NW 23rd
  • Streetcar & bus service to downtown
