Stewart Manor

Community ID: 12847

About This Community

An intimate village of just under five hundred homes, this Long Island community is famous for its tree-canopied streets, English-style cottages, and one of the highest-rated school districts in the state. Generations of families have stayed put, drawn by the rare combination of small-village atmosphere and a thirty-minute LIRR commute to Penn Station.

Local life centers around the village green, the train station, and the historic library. Weekends bring farmers markets in the spring, a beloved fall arts festival, and a community-run holiday tree lighting in December.

Why Buyers Choose This Area
  • Top-decile public school district
  • Walkable village center with independent shops
  • LIRR direct service to Manhattan
  • Strong long-term home value appreciation
