Boca Pointe

A private country club community of 28 distinct villages

Country Club Living, Reimagined

Boca Pointe is one of South Florida's most established private country club communities, set on more than 500 acres just west of Boca Raton's downtown. Inside the manned gate are 28 distinct villages, each with its own architectural style and homeowner association, ranging from intimate villa enclaves to estate homes on the golf course.

The club itself has been recently reinvented with a $40 million renovation: a brand-new clubhouse, two restaurants, a fitness and wellness center, a tennis and pickleball complex, an aquatics center, and the recently re-grassed championship course. Membership is optional, with multiple membership tiers including a sports-only category.

Lifestyle Highlights
  • 28 unique villages within the gated community
  • Championship golf course (optional membership)
  • Tennis & pickleball complex with stadium court
  • Resort-style aquatics & wellness center
