Buckhead

Atlanta's luxury district

About Buckhead

Buckhead is Atlanta's uptown - a luxury district anchored by world-class shopping, fine dining, Class-A office towers, and some of the South's most gracious historic estates. The neighborhood balances cosmopolitan urban density with leafy residential streets where many of the city's most established families have lived for generations.

Residential offerings range from sleek high-rise condos to multi-million-dollar estate homes on parklike lots. Top private schools, the High Museum-adjacent arts corridor, and a growing collection of Michelin-recommended restaurants make Buckhead a perennial favorite for executives, professional athletes, and luxury-home buyers relocating to the Southeast.
