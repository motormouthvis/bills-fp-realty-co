About Lake Nona

Lake Nona is a 17-square-mile master-planned community in southeast Orlando, home to a renowned Medical City, an innovation campus, a USTA national campus, and over fifteen distinct residential villages with options from the $300s to the $3M+ range.

Builders currently selling include Toll Brothers, Pulte, Taylor Morrison, and several boutique luxury developers. Most villages share a community pool, fitness center, and miles of trails connected by autonomous shuttle.
