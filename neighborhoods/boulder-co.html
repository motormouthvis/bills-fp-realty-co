Neighborhood Description

Downtown Boulder is a walkable, bike-friendly community at the foot of the Flatirons, anchored by the Pearl Street pedestrian mall, the University of Colorado, and a thriving startup and outdoor-industry scene. Tree-lined residential streets sit just blocks from craft breweries, farm-to-table restaurants, and direct trail access to Chautauqua and the Foothills.

What You'll Find
  • Walkscore of 90+ with daily-needs walkability
  • Highly-rated Boulder Valley School District schools
  • Top-tier breweries, coffee shops and a year-round farmers market
  • Quick access to the Flatiron trails and University of Colorado campus
