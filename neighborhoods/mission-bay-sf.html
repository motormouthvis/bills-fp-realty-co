Mission Bay

San Francisco's newest waterfront neighborhood

A Built-From-Scratch SF Neighborhood

Mission Bay is the most ambitious urban-redevelopment project in modern San Francisco history. Once a 303-acre rail yard, it has been transformed over the past two decades into a dense, walkable waterfront neighborhood anchored by UCSF Medical Center, the Chase Center arena, and one of the largest biotech clusters on the West Coast.

Housing is overwhelmingly mid- and high-rise condos, with a growing collection of waterfront townhouse projects. Residents enjoy the Bay Trail, Mission Creek Park, dog parks, and direct Muni Metro and Caltrain service into downtown. Recent additions include the UCSF Children's Hospital expansion and several Class-A office campuses for Salesforce, Uber, and major life-science tenants.

What Sets It Apart
  • Waterfront promenade along the Bay Trail
  • Chase Center home of the Golden State Warriors
  • UCSF research campus and hospital
  • Direct Caltrain & Muni T-Line service
