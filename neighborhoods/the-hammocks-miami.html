The Hammocks Community

Located at 9050 Hammocks Blvd

A South Florida Original

The Hammocks is one of the largest planned communities in Miami-Dade County, built across more than 1,200 acres of former pine-rockland and farmland. The community is organized around 18 interconnected lakes, with most homes sitting on the water or one street back.

Inside the gates you'll find more than two dozen subdivisions ranging from townhome enclaves built in the early 1980s to newer luxury single-family villages with custom homes on quarter-acre lots. The community recreation district operates two pools, a fitness center, lighted tennis courts, and a sports complex with soccer, basketball and pickleball.
