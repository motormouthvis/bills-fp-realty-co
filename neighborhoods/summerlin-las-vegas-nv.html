Summerlin

22,500 acres of master-planned living at the foot of Red Rock Canyon

A Mountain-Adjacent City Within Las Vegas

Summerlin is one of the nation's top-selling master-planned communities and the crown jewel of Howard Hughes' Southern Nevada portfolio. Built directly against the red sandstone walls of Red Rock Canyon, Summerlin spans 22,500 acres and is home to more than 100,000 residents across 30+ villages.

The community has its own downtown - an open-air shopping, dining, and entertainment district - plus a Triple-A baseball stadium, a championship hockey practice facility, two golf courses ranked among Nevada's best, and the largest park system in the state at 250+ parks.
