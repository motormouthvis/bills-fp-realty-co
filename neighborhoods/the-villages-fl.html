The Villages

America's friendliest hometown for 55+ active adults

A Community Like No Other

The Villages is the largest 55+ active-adult community in the United States, with more than 80,000 homes spread across three counties of Central Florida. Residents zip between three town squares on golf carts via more than 100 miles of dedicated cart paths.

Every evening, all three town squares feature free live entertainment, and residents have access to more than 50 executive golf courses, dozens of pools, a polo grounds, and over 2,700 community clubs ranging from astronomy to zydeco dancing. Healthcare is anchored by a major teaching hospital partner on campus.
