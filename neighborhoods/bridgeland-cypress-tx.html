Bridgeland

11,400 acres of lakes, trails, and curated nature in Northwest Houston

A Master-Planned Community Built Around Water

Bridgeland is one of the top-selling master-planned communities in the United States, set on 11,400 acres in northwest Houston. The community is organized around an interconnected system of lakes, waterways, and trails, with more than 3,000 acres preserved as parks, lakes, and natural reserves.

Builders include Highland Homes, Perry Homes, Trendmaker, Toll Brothers, Westin, Coventry, and David Weekley, with homes from the upper $300s into the $2M+ range across more than a dozen villages. The Lakeland Village Center, Parkland Village's Activity Center, and the brand-new Prairieland Village are the main amenity hubs.

Lifestyle Highlights
  • 900+ acres of lakes & waterways
  • 60+ miles of trails for hiking, biking and kayaking
  • Three resort-style activity centers
  • Cy-Fair ISD schools on-campus
