Rancho Mission Viejo

A new generation of ranch-inspired living in South Orange County

A Ranch Reimagined

Rancho Mission Viejo is South Orange County's premier master-planned community, set on land that has been a working California ranch for more than 130 years. Today, the ranch is being thoughtfully developed village by village, with two-thirds of the original land preserved as open space.

The flagship village of Esencia anchors the community with two resort-style pools, a hilltop clubhouse, twin guest houses, miles of trails, and walking access to K-8 schools. Sendero, Gavilan (55+), Rienda, and the newer villages each offer their own amenity collection, all connected by a community-wide trail system.
