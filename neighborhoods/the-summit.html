The Summit

About The Summit

Tucked into the Blue Ridge Mountains, The Summit is a private gated community known for ridge-top custom homes, sunset views over the French Broad valley, and a deeply quiet atmosphere. Homes are spaced generously on lots that average just over two acres, and most are oriented to capture the long-range mountain views.

Residents have access to a private clubhouse, walking trails, and a member-only fly-fishing creek. The community has a part-time concierge program for seasonal residents and is a popular second-home destination for buyers from Atlanta, Charlotte, and Charleston.

Highlights
  • Long-range Blue Ridge mountain views
  • Gated entry and 24/7 patrol
  • Two-acre minimum lots
  • Private clubhouse, trails and fly-fishing creek
