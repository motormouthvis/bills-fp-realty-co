Eagle Ridge

A quiet established neighborhood with mature trees and a strong HOA

About Eagle Ridge

Eagle Ridge is a private community of just over 300 single-family homes, built progressively between 1986 and 2002. The neighborhood is known for its low turnover, large wooded lots, and an exceptionally engaged community association that maintains the entrance, common areas, and the community pool and tennis complex.

Streets curve gently through hardwood forest and connect to the regional W&OD trail. Most homes sit on half-acre to one-acre lots and feature classic brick or cedar-shake exteriors. Residents typically commute to nearby office parks or take the express bus to DC.

Community Amenities
  • Community pool, tennis, and pickleball courts
  • Wooded common areas with walking paths
  • Strong HOA with active social calendar
  • Top-rated public elementary in district
