Trilogy at Vistancia

Shea Homes' flagship 55+ resort community in the Sonoran Desert foothills

Resort Living, Every Day

Trilogy at Vistancia is consistently ranked among the top 55+ communities in the country. Built into the Sonoran foothills on the northwest edge of the Phoenix metro, the community wraps around Gary Panks-designed golf, more than thirty miles of hiking and biking trails, and the 35,000-square-foot Kiva Club resort center.

Inside the Kiva you'll find an indoor lap pool, an outdoor resort pool, a spa, the Cafe Solaz restaurant, an artisan studio, a movement studio, and a teaching kitchen. Outdoor amenities include pickleball, bocce, tennis, golf, and a network of trails that connect directly to the Sonoran Preserve.
