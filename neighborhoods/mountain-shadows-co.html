Mountain Shadows

A beloved Front Range neighborhood with Garden of the Gods on its doorstep

Foothills Living, City Convenience

Mountain Shadows is one of Colorado Springs' most desirable established neighborhoods, with homes climbing the foothills directly above Garden of the Gods Park. The community is famous for big mountain views, mature ponderosa pines, and a strong sense of identity — residents rebuilt the neighborhood together after the 2012 Waldo Canyon fire and the recovery has produced one of the most cohesive communities in the city.

Homes range from preserved 1970s ranches to award-winning rebuilds and new custom builds. Trails connect the neighborhood directly to Garden of the Gods, Ute Valley Park, and the Section 16 trail system. Schools include some of the best-rated public elementary and middle schools in Academy District 20.

Highlights
  • Direct trail access to Garden of the Gods & Ute Valley
  • Award-winning Academy District 20 schools
  • Long-range Front Range views
  • Active community association
