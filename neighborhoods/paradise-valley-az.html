Paradise Valley

Arizona's most exclusive luxury enclave

Where Phoenix's Most Coveted Estates Reside

Tucked between Camelback and Mummy Mountain, Paradise Valley is a 16-square-mile incorporated town of just under 14,000 residents and over 6,000 luxury estates. Founded in 1961 to preserve a low-density resort and equestrian character, the town zones primarily for one-acre lots and maintains no commercial corridors outside its resort district.

Toll Brothers' Paradise Valley model collection sits along Tatum Boulevard and features four single-level luxury floor plans. Model homes are open daily for self-guided tours, with a sales gallery anchoring the entrance.
